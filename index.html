About Me

Who is Aawart KC?

My name is Aawart KC. I'm an 18-year-old security researcher from Nepal who has discovered a few hundred bugs across some of the internet's most critical systems.

I do bug bounty as a hobby, usually 2–3 sessions a week. It's not a 24/7 grind, it's something I genuinely enjoy. I look for real-impact vulnerabilities and always report responsibly.

I've reported security issues to some of the world's most recognized organizations. Every responsible disclosure is a small step toward a safer internet.

I'm living in Nepal, enjoying and writing about my findings on my blog.
